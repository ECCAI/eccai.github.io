Program

13:30pm-14:00pm Registration & Welcome Refreshments!
14:00pm-14:10pm Opening Remarks
Introduction to the organizers and the goals of the workshop
14:10pm-14:40pm Keynote 1
Dr. Wentao Xie (The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology)
Topic: Edge Device Done Right - Unleashing Health Measuring Potential of Edge Devices with Smart Sensing Design
14:40pm-15:20pm [Session 1]
Cloud-edge collaboration for AI
Industry and academic talks on AI applications empowered by cloud-edge collaboration. Interactive session encouraging participants to share insights and experiences.
15:20pm-15:50pm Keynote 2
Speaker: Dr. Mu Yuan (The Chinese University of Hong Kong)
Keynote presentation on cutting-edge research and applications in AI, highlighting collaborative efforts and future directions.
15:50pm-16:20pm [Session 2]
AI for cloud-edge collaboration
Discussion on AI-based and LLM-based agent techniques for cloud-edge collaboration, including network optimization, bandwidth control, fault prediction, and performance enhancement. Sharing the latest research outcomes and practical insights.
16:20pm-17:00pm [Session 3]
Poster Session
Miscellaneous including the deployment experiences and case studies, specific applications of AI on edge devices and cloud. The session encourages all the participants including the previous oral participants to engage in social conversation and discussion.
17:00pm-17:30pm Closing Remarks
Outlook on the future of Edge-Cloud Collaboration + AI integration
Summary of key takeaways from the sessions. Final thoughts on emerging trends, opportunities, and challenges in integrating AI with edge-cloud systems. Open discussion on potential collaboration and future events.
